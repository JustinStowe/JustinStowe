A Sample of my Projects:

SEI Cafe

This app is a simulation of a merchant website that offers the ability to add items to a virtual shopping cart and check out. The purpose of the app is entertainment and proof of concept, it does not provide actual e-commerce services.

My Movie Tracker

This app is designed for movie enthusiasts. Allowing them to organize their movie watching activities in one place. Users can keep track of movies they intend to watch and movies they have already seen, befriend fellow movie enthusiasts and add personal comments on each film as well.


Photography Business Portal

This app is a comprehensive platform that allows users to showcase their portfolios, manage their clients and efficiently deliver photo-shoots. While currently under development, I aims to constantly expand its features and capabilities.
